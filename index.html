Our Story

A small-scale rabbitry
with a big heart

Blush Meadow Rabbitry was born from a simple belief: that every Holland Lop deserves to be raised slowly, gently, and with genuine love. Nestled on Long Island, New York, we are a home-based rabbitry built on the conviction that quality always comes before quantity.

We keep our litters intentionally small — because every kit deserves daily handling, individual attention, and plenty of lap time from birth. By the time your bunny comes home, they already know what it feels like to be loved.

How to Adopt

Our litters are small and our waitlists fill fast. Here's exactly how the process works — from first hello to bringing your bunny home.

01
💌

Reach Out

When the waitlist opens (announced on Instagram first), email us or send a DM. Spots are limited — blushmeadowrabbitry@gmail.com or @blushmeadowrabbitry.

02
📝

Submit an Application

Fill out our short adoption questionnaire. We personally review every family to make sure our bunnies go to homes where they'll truly thrive.

03
🤍

Reserve & Meet Your Kit

Once approved and your non-refundable deposit is confirmed, you'll receive introduction photos of your kit. Follow us on Instagram to watch them grow through our regular videos and updates.

04
🐇

Pick-up takes place in Deer Park, Long Island. Ask us about our take-home kit — thoughtfully curated to give your bunny the smoothest, most comfortable transition into your home.

Diet & Nutrition

Unlimited timothy hay forms 80% of a Holland Lop's diet. We provide a full nutritional guide and our recommended pellet & veggie schedule with every adoption.

🏠

Housing Setup

Holland Lops thrive in exercise pens with room to binky. Learn our recommended pen setup, flooring, and enrichment tips.

🏥

Finding a Rabbit-Savvy Vet

Bunnies don't require vaccines, but having a rabbit-savvy vet on call is essential. GI stasis is a common and serious condition — knowing your vet before an emergency makes all the difference. We'll help you find one on Long Island.

✂️

Grooming

Holland Lops need weekly brushing and monthly nail trims. We'll show you our favorite tools and gentle technique at pickup.
